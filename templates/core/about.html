About JBAC

Jesus Believers Association Council

A community platform for registrations, trusted directory access, and ministry coordination across Andhra Pradesh.

Purpose

JBAC exists to maintain a secure network of believers, pastors, churches, students, and Christian organizations. The platform supports registration workflows, searchable public ministry records, and community-wide updates.

Bilingual access

The interface is written for both English and Telugu readers so the platform works well for local congregations and statewide outreach teams.

Community directory

Pastor and church data can be approved and published for search while protecting confidential member registrations.

Role-specific registration

Each user type gets a form tailored to its data requirements, from students to full ministry organizations.

Operational simplicity

Django admin gives JBAC staff one place to review records, manage updates, and keep the system current.
